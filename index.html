Heysen preference simulator

Live results from the ECSA feed, plus a preference flow simulator you can play with.

How it works

Candidates get eliminated from the bottom and their votes get redistributed. Use the sliders to set where you think those preferences go — or just pick a preset and tweak from there.

You can type vote numbers directly (e.g. 500) or percentages (25%). Don't stress about getting totals to exactly 100%, it'll sort itself out.

Presets

  • 2022 Actual — how preferences actually flowed in Heysen last time
  • 2022 Postal Lean — same but shifted for how postal voters behaved differently (more LIB, less GRN)
  • Win scenarios — what-if scenarios where Greens get up

The bar at the bottom compares your sim against the actual count from scrutineers. It updates as postals come in.

Just a heads up — this is for playing around with, not an actual prediction. The preference numbers are based on 2022 and might not reflect 2026. Not affiliated with ECSA.
